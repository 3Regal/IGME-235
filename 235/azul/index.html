Azul

What is Azul?

In Azul, players take turns drafting tiles from a supply and placing them in
their palace. Players score based on how they place their tiles, and earn bonus
points for building specific patterns. Incomplete sets of tiles end up damaging
one's score, making Azul a game about careful planning and keeping an eye
on your opponents' palaces.

User caggabekingaf commented,

"Cutthroat at 2 players."

This is because when the game is played with only 2 players,
drafting choices heavily influence what your opponent can take.
This results in a game not only about your board of tiles, but also
very much about what your opponent is trying to accomplish on theirs.
